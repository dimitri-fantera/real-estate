# DealScope: The AI Underwriting Platform for Mid-Market Real Estate Investors

## One-Liner

Institutional-grade deal analysis for the 2M+ small and mid-market real estate investors who currently underwrite deals in spreadsheets.

---

## The Problem

Over 68% of institutional CRE investors use AI-assisted underwriting, but the 2M+ investors operating 10-500 units are stuck with:

- **Spreadsheet hell**: Every deal gets a custom Excel model, built from scratch or copied from a course. Error-prone, slow, non-standardized.
- **Fragmented data**: Rent comps, property taxes, insurance estimates, cap rates, and market trends live in 10+ different places (Zillow, CoStar fragments, county assessor sites, LoopNet, Census data). Pulling it together takes hours per deal.
- **No institutional memory**: Past deals, passed-on deals, and lessons learned vanish. There's no system of record for an investor's decision history.
- **Blind spots on risk**: Small operators can't model interest rate scenarios, demographic shifts, or regulatory changes the way institutions do.

The result: mid-market investors either (a) overpay because they can't underwrite fast enough to compete, or (b) miss deals because analysis takes too long.

---

## The Solution

**DealScope** is an AI-native underwriting platform that turns a property listing URL or address into a full investment analysis in under 60 seconds.

### Core Product

1. **Instant Deal Analysis** -- Paste a listing URL or enter an address. DealScope pulls rent comps, tax records, insurance estimates, market vacancy rates, and historical appreciation data. It generates a pro forma with cash-on-cash return, IRR, cap rate, and DSCR -- instantly.

2. **AI Risk Scoring** -- Every deal gets a 0-100 risk score based on market fundamentals (population growth, job growth, supply pipeline), property-specific factors (age, condition, deferred maintenance signals), and financing risk (rate sensitivity analysis).

3. **Portfolio Intelligence** -- As investors analyze more deals, DealScope learns their criteria and surfaces deals that match. It also tracks portfolio-level metrics: concentration risk, geographic diversification, weighted average returns.

4. **Deal Flow Pipeline** -- A CRM-like pipeline for tracking deals from discovery through close. Attach notes, share analyses with partners or lenders, and maintain an institutional-quality deal log.

5. **Lender-Ready Packages** -- One-click export of underwriting packages formatted for bank/credit union submission. Saves 5-10 hours per deal on lender prep.
