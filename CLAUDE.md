# CLAUDE.md

## Project Context

This repo is an idea-stage exploration for a YC-track startup in real estate. We have not committed to a specific product yet — we are in the discovery phase.

## Key Files

- `USER_PERSONAS.md` — 67 distinct personas across 12 categories touching ~14M+ individuals in the US. Use this to understand who works in real estate and what their pain points are.
- `PaulGraham.md` — Paul Graham's framework for finding startup ideas, applied to real estate. Follow these principles when evaluating or generating ideas.
- `PRODUCT_IDEA.md` — An earlier product concept (DealScope). Treat as one possibility, not a decision.

## Guiding Principles (from PG's essay)

- **Notice problems, don't brainstorm.** Good ideas come from observing what's broken, not from ideation sessions.
- **Scratch your own itch.** Prioritize problems with strong founder-market fit.
- **Start narrow and deep ("well" ideas).** A small group with intense need beats a large group with mild interest.
- **Follow the schlep.** Tedious, fragmented, unsexy problems are the best opportunities because others avoid them.
- **Talk to people.** Ask "what do you waste time on?" not "would you use X?"
- **Don't over-plan.** Validate the core problem before designing the full platform.

## When Helping with This Repo

- Reference the user personas when discussing target customers or pain points.
- Apply PG's framework when evaluating ideas — does it solve a real problem? Is it narrow and deep? Is there schlep others avoid?
- We are targeting YC. Ideas should be ambitious, defensible, and have a clear wedge into a large market.
- Keep things scrappy and focused. Avoid over-engineering plans before validating the core insight.
